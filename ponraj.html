Profile Summary

Dr. Ponraj Asirwadam Paulraj is a mathematician with research interests spanning generalized topological spaces, bitopology, H-hyperconnected structures, neutrosophic systems, stability theory, topological data analysis, multi-agent systems, AI in healthcare, and robust decision-making frameworks. His recent work focuses on connecting rigorous mathematical structures with real-world AI applications, particularly in healthcare, navigation, genomic data analysis, and intelligent risk modelling.
